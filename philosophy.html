Our Philosophy

01

AI is a game of singles.

Much attention is given to futuristic applications of AI such as self-driving cars, computer vision and the like. However, we believe AI is much more relevant to most companies in core business operations such as demand and revenue forecasting, inventory planning, network management and churn reduction. The benefit comes not from a single application but from making lots of improvements in many areas.

02

AI is most impactful in real-time.

No doubt an executive or team can gain insights from AI-driven dashboards and analytics (and Machinify has amazing dashboard tools). However, the greatest impact can be had when AI is utilized to make thousands or millions of real-time predictions as a critical component of a company’s products and processes.

03

Speed and low marginal cost matter.

When it takes a long time and a lot of people to create an AI intervention, a company will be hesitant to commit to a project for fear of being wrong. However, when the actual and opportunity cost of trial is low, a company is freed to try many things - keeping with our baseball analogy, to have many more at-bats.

04

We must automate low-value tasks.

Data scientists are a valuable commodity. In order to best leverage their skills, we must to free them from the unleveraged labor and allow them to focus their time on creatively applying their skills to as many problems/questions as possible.
