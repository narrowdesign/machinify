Our Partnership Model

We offer our partners a high level of support and guidance, balanced with a great deal of autonomy and control. We blend this way of working into a flexible economic framework that aligns with each partner's unique business needs.

We will work with your team according to the following steps of development, deployment and operation:

Build.

We take responsibility for the initial build and delivery, in full collaboration with your team, so knowledge is transferred in a hands-on contextual manner.

Operate.

We then operationalize the AI modeling and production solution while continuing with the training and transfer of knowledge.

Transfer.

We transfer operational control to your team, giving you full autonomy and control to create and deploy new AI-driven products.

Our economic framework is a packaged solution of software and services.

Software.

We provide usage-based access to our AI platform, including GUI for model creation, production-ready APIs, and charting tools that can be used on a standalone basis or can be integrated into existing dashboards. Machinify can operate in our own multi-tenant cloud environment or can be installed on your own virtual private cloud.

Data Science.

Our data science experts are available to help you solve actual problems in your business. The Build-Operate-Transfer model reduces risk and accelerates time to market, while giving you full control at the end of the process.

Data Engineering.

In some instances - where the data structures or modeling are especially complex, or there are embedded software or model deployments - we offer highly skilled data engineers to ensure a fast and effective deployment.
