Realizing the potential of AI

We Are Machinify

Machinify helps companies build AI-driven smart products that turn data into dollars by delivering three unique capabilities: We enable our partners to go from raw data to deployed product in minutes, not months.

We improve the productivity of data scientists by 10x, by allowing them to focus on the hardest, highest leverage problems. We unleash AI to become a broadly available capability across your organization.

OUR PERSPECTIVE ON AI

AI is a game of singles. The greatest benefit to a company comes from being deployed in many areas and uses cases across the organization. Speed, democratization and collaboration are essential. A company must be able to iterate and experiment in situations where the ROI is not known in advance. The marginal cost of experimentation must be low, and AI must be be accessible by domain experts across the org. Data scientists should focus on high value challenges, not the manual labor of scripting. The org’s collective advances should build on one another and be available to all.

THE AI ADOPTION CHALLENGE

Manual labor. Developing AI today is a slow, manual process. Valuable thinkers spend their time writing throwaway scripts. Working in Silos: Each new AI model starts from zero. Expertise and learning stay in peoples’ brains. Different disciplines lack a common platform for collaboration. Deployment is Arduous: Data and AI architecture very hard to implement in a production setting. AI insights remain locked in charts, not deployed into processes.

MACHINIFY UNLEASHES AI

We provide technology and expertise that enable companies to conceive, build and deploy smart products and processes. Automation of time-consuming tasks shrinks development time from months to days. Freed from scripting, data scientists are able to focus on high leverage problems/projects that can have the largest impact on your company Machinify platform makes AI accessible to domain experts in multiple functional areas. A company can manage and monitor AI projects across the org. Close partnership insures success.

MACHINIFY PLATFORM

AI Cloud:

Collects and automatically structures data from any source. Data transformation accomplished via creation of formulas. AI Cloud automatically generates the underlying data eng code.

AI Studio:

AI-assisted “compiler” enables models to be built in minutes. All underlying code automatically generated. Change management and feature library enable fast collaboration.

AI Runtime:

Deploy data infra and model code with a single button. Model available via a simple API integration. Realtime scoring, five 9’s reliability; automated batch scoring.

WHO WE ARE

Prasanna Ganesan, Founder and CEO.

Prasanna is one of the great minds in Silicon Valley. After receiving his PhD in Computer Science at Stanford, he co-founded VUDU in 2005 (acquired by Walmart 2010). His pioneering work as CTO resulted in over 30 patents and his receiving the 2013 Home Entertainment Visionary award. Prasanna is the recipient of the President of India Gold Medal for his academic accomplishments and also holds the distinction of being the #1 ranked student entering the Indian Institute of Technology.

Alain Rossmann, Co-Founder and Chairman.

Alain has been creating world-changing technology going all the way back to the Mac team at Apple, where he worked directly for Steve Jobs. Following that, Alain went on to found Radius, C-Cube, OpenWave and co-found VUDU. Among his many accomplishments, he introduced web-browsing to phones and collectively created several billion dollars of market value. As Chairman, Alain provides valuable strategic guidance to Machinify.

Tony Miranz, Co-Founder and President.

Tony is among the best in Silicon Valley at forging partnerships that bring new products and services to global markets at scale. He was the founding CEO of VUDU which ignited the digital distribution of Hollywood movies giving rise to Netflix, Hulu, Apple-TV digital deliver services.

Edward Lichty, COO.

Edward has almost 20 years of experience of successfully developing innovative strategies, creating and managing successful partnerships, and creating highly effective organizations. After leading corp dev at TiVo for many years, Edward joined Prasanna, Alain and Tony at VUDU, where he served as VP of business development and then COO. In 2010 Edward led the sale of VUDU to Walmart, after which he served as GM running the VUDU business for 2 years. Most recently he led strategy and partnerships at Medium, working closely with Ev Williams to take Medium from beta stage to over 80M monthly uniques.
